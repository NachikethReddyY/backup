## DOCUMENT PURPOSE

This document captures the TELOS method of articulating a person's personal context so that they, and AI, can better understand them and help them improve. Pay attention to the items and the concepts here, and especially on how they work together.

The conceptual path is Problems -> Mission -> (Narratives) -> Goals -> Challenges -> Strategies —> Projects -> Journal. This means that any project you're doing can be mapped all the way back up to the problem(s) you're trying to solve. This gives you transparency and explainability in everything you're doing so you don't end up busy for days/months/years without being able to remember why you're doing what you're doing.

Other sections that aren't those core sections are additional context to support them.

- 2026-04-02: At Autodesk Singapore for Autodesk Singapore Hackathlon. Won the people's choise award. 
🏆 Today, we won the People's Choice Award at the Autodesk Singapore Hackathon 2026.
Six weeks of holidays and I was already restless. When I saw the chance to build alongside industry professionals at Autodesk, I jumped at it. Best decision of my holidays.

🤝 The Partner Agents
I arrived knowing nobody. I left having learned from some genuinely brilliant people:
Zhu Wensheng (Wilson) – my team leader, deep knowledge of Partner Web Services (PWS) and API architecture
Jagan Mohan Rao Appala – data scientist and great ideator
Hafil Noer Zachiary – new Autodesk engineer, incredibly AI-forward — uses it as a true force multiplier
Sean Su – graduating SP student who gave me a real preview of Year 2 and beyond

💡 What we built
Autodesk's Partner Web Services connects manufacturers, resellers & customers — but quotations still required a manual phone call. We fixed that.
We built an AI layer for instant quotations and bulk orders, plus a weather API for logistics and a full product catalog browser.
My contributions: product list, search & catalog functions — plus the full demo video, featuring an ElevenLabs voiceover in a New Zealand accent and a SpongeBob soundtrack that somehow worked perfectly. 🎬
☕ Equally important: the perks.
Free-flow coffee. Vending machines full of drinks. Milo on demand. 🍫 Endless snacks. The workspace was modern, open, and the kind of place that makes you want to stay and build. I genuinely hope to come back here after I graduate — the culture, the people, the office. It all felt like somewhere I could grow.

🏆 And yes — we won the People's Choice Award.
Every team was pushing to build something that mattered. The energy was electric.

To my team: thank you.
