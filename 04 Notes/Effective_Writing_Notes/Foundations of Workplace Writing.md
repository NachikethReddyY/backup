# Foundations of Workplace Writing

## PAC Principle
The bedrock of written communication, influencing content, structure, and language.

- **Purpose**: Objectives/aims (e.g., to explain, propose, instruct).
- **Audience**: Intended readers (e.g., decision-makers, project partners).
- **Context**: The situation or background information that builds meaning.

## The 5Cs of Communication
A checklist for effective writing:
- Complete
- Concise
- Clear
- Courteous
- Correct

## Types of Written Communication
- Project Proposals
- Technical Reports
- Emails
- Memos & Letters
